## Key Benefits of Compression

* **Reduced Latency:** Faster response times for users.
* **Increased Throughput:** Handling more concurrent user requests per second.
* **Cost Efficiency:** Lower hardware requirements by reducing the number of GPUs needed for deployment.

---

## How Quantization Works

Quantization involves reducing the numerical precision of a model’s weights. For example, this might involve converting from floating point 16 (2 bytes per parameter) to integer 8 (1 byte) or integer 4 (0.5 bytes). This allows massive models to fit into significantly less VRAM without sacrificing accuracy, as demonstrated by benchmarks showing less than 1% degradation.
